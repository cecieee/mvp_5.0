Tasks

Task 1
Create a GoogleApps@IEEE account:

GoogleApps@IEEE provides a lot of opportunities to IEEE members without causing any additional cost. Members gain a robust capability for messaging, with access to their email, calendar, and contact services along with providing security by running an automatic scanning for viruses. Besides, it offers more privacy and credibility to the user. Now it's your turn to create your IEEE mail ID.

Task 2
Follow IEEE on all social media pages and upload screenshots:

Follow the IEEE SB CEC social media pages: To redeem points for this task, the members have to follow the IEEE SB CEC page on Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn, and provide three screenshots of having done the same in the google form presented to them. Student members who do not own any one or two of these accounts must follow the page with whatever available account they possess.

Task 3
Create a LinkedIn profile and share your profile

Create a LinkedIn profile, which is the most effective and fastest professional network platform that offers opportunities every day. LinkedIn is a platform connecting new people with their professional designations. It is essentially used to establish necessary relationships with professional aspects and act as a guide to quickly find a job.

Task 4
Make a report based on any IEEE research paper

Choose a research paper from the IEEE Xplore Digital Library.
Read and understand the paper thoroughly.
Write a report summarizing the key points, methodology, results, and your insights.
