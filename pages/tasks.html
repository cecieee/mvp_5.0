Tasks

Task 1
Set up GoogleApps@IEEE account:

GoogleApps@IEEE provides numerous opportunities to IEEE volunteers by rendering them a robust capability for messaging with access to a unique IEEE email address, Google Drive, calendar, and contact services without any additional charges. It lends the user with a high level of secrecy and eminence. The participants are expected to create an account in GoogleApps@IEEE and submit their IEEE mail-id along with the screenshot of the account via the Google Form provided.

Task 2
Create a LinkedIn profile and share your profile ID:

Creating a LinkedIn profile helps in expanding your network and to enhance professional connections. It offers a platform to showcase your skills, experiences and aspirations. Here you'll create a LinkedIn profile and make at least 20 connections. After that, you can share the URL of your profile through a Google Form. It's a great way to network professionally and open up new opportunities. Good luck.

Task 3
Explore a paper from IEEE Xplore library and submit the report.

Access the IEEE Xplore Digital Library at ieeexplore.ieee.org to find a paper relevant to your field of interest. Read the chosen paper with a focus on comprehending the problem addressed, methodology used, findings, and conclusions. Craft a report that summarizes the key points of the paper, discusses its significance, and incorporates your insights and analysis

Task 4
Make five IEEE connections in IEEE Collabratec.

With IEEE Collabratec you can connect with global technology professionals by technical interests, IEEE- affiliations, authorship, region etc. It is an integrated platform to network, collaborate and create with technology experts. Participants must create a profile, make five connections in IEEE Collabratec and share the URL of their profile in the Google form below.

Task 5
Follow IEEE SB CEC pages on all social media platforms

This task aims to enhance your involvement with IEEE by expanding your reach across their various official social media platforms. The participants should follow IEEE SB CEC pages on Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn and attach the screenshots to the provided google form to show that you have done the same. Participants who do not have a profile on the mentioned platforms can complete the task using an account they have access to

Task 6
Plan an event from the vision and mission based on an IEEE Society.

The participants are expected to learn about IEEE's Societies, read through the vision and mission of each society and provide a brief plan idea to conduct an event on the basis of any chosen society. Participants are allowed to expand their creative vision and come up with ideas related to the chosen mission. A brief description on your intended event and it's execution plan is to be drafted and submitted in the Google form

Task 7
Add IEEE and Society Membership to Cart:

Add your IEEE membership to the cart, but you do not need to pay or checkout. Instead, upload a screenshot of the cart via the submission link provided.

Task 8
Purchase a free webinar:

Login to ieee.org and purchase a free webinar. Submit the details of webinar name, ID and a small abstract about the webinar content through the submission link.
