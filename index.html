About

IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Chengannur (60451) is organizing MVP - Micro Volunteering Programme for IEEE members, to focus on the development of volunteering skills, to enable networking and socialising with fellow members from the Student Branch. The program looks forward to build a trailblazing platform for the student leaders to interact across regions to share their knowledge, find their best applications, cultivate different ideas and much more.

Rules

  1. To enroll in the volunteering program,the student must have a valid IEEE membership ID
  2. Online tasks will be provided to the volunteers on alternate days.
  3. Reports of the completed task should be submitted via the Google Form that will be provided along with each task.
  4. The successful completion of each task will reward you with points.

Leaderboard

The leaderboard will be updated on a task basis. The top 3 volunteers will be awarded with exciting prizes.
